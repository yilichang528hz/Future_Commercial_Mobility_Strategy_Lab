# Assumptions and Limitations

This prototype is designed as a personal portfolio demonstration for future commercial mobility strategy planning.

## Assumptions

- Some technology readiness scores are demo assumptions.
- Some roadmap items are scenario-based examples.
- International trend data requires local validation before applying to Taiwan.
- Energy transition timing depends on vehicle availability, infrastructure, policy, customer routes, service readiness, and TCO conditions.
- ADAS and automation claims require technical and regulatory confirmation.

## Limitations

- This prototype does not represent official Toyota, HINO, Hotai Motor, dealer, customer, or government positions.
- Vehicle specifications, launch plans, pricing, subsidies, charging infrastructure, and regulatory status must be verified before formal use.
- Autonomous driving and ADAS materials should avoid overclaiming safety outcomes.
- ESG and carbon estimates require verified emission factors and enterprise-approved accounting methods.
- The strategy roadmap is a planning framework, not an official product launch schedule.
