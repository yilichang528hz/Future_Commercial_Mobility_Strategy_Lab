## Home-only legal notice block
The Home page top-left or fixed top area must show language-specific legal notice text. This block is a Home-only element and must not be duplicated inside every module section:

- zh: `著作權所有 © 2026 張義豊(Yi-Li, Chang) 保留所有權利。`
- en: `Copyright © 2026 張義豊(Yi-Li, Chang) All Rights Reserved.`
- ja: `著作権所有 © 2026 張義豊(Yi-Li, Chang) 無断転載を禁じます。`

This Home legal notice must update when the language changes.

## Home-only Creative Motivation block
Add this Home-only section after the Hero or before the first module cards. Do not copy it into module phases:

### zh title
`創作動機`

### zh copy - max 180 Chinese characters
`我希望用商用車商品企劃的角度，將新能源、車聯網、智慧安全、ESG與AI治理轉化為可討論的策略工具，讓技術趨勢回到人、車隊、通路與台灣產業情境。`

### en title
`Creative Motivation`

### en copy
`This lab turns future mobility signals into practical strategy tools for commercial vehicle product planning, connecting energy transition, connected fleets, safety, ESG, AI governance, and Taiwan-based operating contexts.`

### ja title
`制作動機`

### ja copy
`このラボは、商用車の商品企画の視点から、エネルギー転換、コネクテッドフリート、安全、ESG、AIガバナンスを実務的な戦略ツールへ整理します。`

## Tone rule
The motivation text should be reflective and professional. It should not sound like a slogan or self-promotion.
